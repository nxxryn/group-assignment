Pahang.

One of The Theme Park in Genting

Genting SkyWorlds is a movie inspired theme park project at Resorts World Genting, Genting Highlands, Malaysia. The theme park will open in the second quarter of 2021. Formerly called 20th Century Fox World, the park was renamed after reaching a settlement with 21st Century Fox and The Walt Disney Company

Penang.

Bukit Bendera - Flag Hill

Penang Hill is a hill resort comprising a group of peaks on Penang Island, Malaysia. It is located within the Air Itam suburb, 9 km west of the center of George Town. Penang Hill is also known by the Malay name Bukit Bendera, which actually refers to Flagstaff Hill, the most developed peak

Melaka.

Historical Building

A Famosa is a former Portuguese fortress built in Malacca, Malaysia, in 1512. The oldest part of the fortress was a keep. Some time following the Battle of Malacca and the occupation of the city by the Dutch, the keep was destroyed but the outer walls of the fortress were reinforced
